Maybricks Sdn Bhd is one of the Asia's leading brick manufacturers specialising in producing high quality construction clay materials: common solid bricks, common blocks, facing bricks, clay pavers and brick tiles. With its headquarter based in Kulai, Johor; Maybricks is supported by workforce of over 150 people with 2 production plants running 24 hours a day; 7 days a week.

Founded in 1972 as Federal Bricks Industries, it was later renamed Maybricks Sdn Bhd. in 1989. Since incorporation, though Maybricks was one of the very few pioneers in clay bricks manufacturing, its focus on quality and total customer satisfaction has remained consistent until today.

Maybricks' carefully-built reputation has been well acknowledged by regional industrial players and was awarded ISO 9001:2008 in 2006. Today, Maybricks clay products are being widely exported to Australia, Taiwan, Middle East, Europe and even countries with extremely stringent requirements such as Japan and Singapore, which contributes more than 80% of the total Maybricks exports.
