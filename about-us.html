Maybricks Sdn Bhd is one of the leading manufacturers of fine quality clay products in Malaysia. The company manufactures burnt clay products, namely:

  1. Common Solid Bricks
  2. Common 3 Hole Blocks
  3. Common 10 Hole Blocks
  4. M211 Modular

Our products are produced under the most stringent quality control and they are really to cater for Building Materials for new building, home improvement or renovation.
