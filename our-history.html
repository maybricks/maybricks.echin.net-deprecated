Maybricks was incorporated as Federal Bricks Industries in 1972 before it was changed in July 14th, 1989 the name to Maybricks Sdn Bhd.

Its main operation is manufacturing common clay bricks or blocks. The production of Maybricks estimated to be 100 million pieces of common clay bricks per year.

Maybricks authorized capital is RM50 million and paid capital is RM26.2 million.

The current production uses fully 100% German Technology, which gives the same quality comparable to the European bricks. Maybricks is able to provide satisfactory products and services to clients worldwide.

With the work force of 100 over people and a team of experienced brick makers, Maybricks is one of the leading bricks manufacturer in Asia.
